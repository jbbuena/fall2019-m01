Shopping

Is it an L.A. trip without a shopping list? Whether you're into the big designer brands or looking for lowkey, vintage pieces to add to your collection, L.A. will have what you're looking for. There's going to be some big shopping areas that also require big budgets, but L.A. also has its fair share of thriftshop options where you're sure to find some hidden gems. No matter what your style is, make sure to go visit some these places on the list below.

Best shopping spots:

  • Rodeo Drive
  • Venice Beach Boardwalk
  • Melrose Avenue Shopping District
  • The Grove
  • Fashion District DTLA
  • Abbot Kinney Boulevard
